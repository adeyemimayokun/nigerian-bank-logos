# Trademark and Logo Usage

This repository contains software, metadata, and tooling under the MIT License.
The logo artwork is different: each bank, fintech, payment provider, and other
financial institution owns its own trademarks and brand assets.

Using this repository does not grant ownership of any logo or permission to use a
third-party trademark in a way that suggests endorsement, partnership, or
affiliation.

Logo assets should only be added from official sources such as:

- The institution's official website.
- Official brand or media kit pages.
- Official annual reports, investor PDFs, or regulatory documents.
- Other institution-owned domains or files that clearly publish the asset.

Maintainers may remove any asset when its source cannot be verified, the brand
owner requests removal, or the logo is outdated.
